About Me

I am a Spokane based Graphic Designer, Videographer, and Photographer. I clearly have my foot in just about every door in the design world haha. My journey began back in 2017 when I was thrown into a huge role of Media Leader at my local church. Since then I have expanded my horizons working with companies and individuals alike. My passion has grown and my skill set has expanded. I love what I do and I love working with others who have a passion for what they do.
